Welcome to the Financial Transparency and Accountability dashboard for the city of Des Moines, Washington. The department of Finance provides oversight of the city’s budgeting, planning, operations and debt management function including funding the capital improvement needs of the municipality through the responsible issuance of municipal debt, as well as determines and approves structures, allocates terms and conditions for all city liabilities, maintains records of bonded indebtedness and the liquidity portfolio, and ensures payment of debt.
